Youtube, The new Study Source

Introduction

Many instructors and parents still consider online streams to be a platform for entertainment or killing time, even nowadays. However, online platforms such as YouTube Channels could be promising instructional tools for students and people who have graduated from school a long time ago. The online resources are accessible twenty-four hours a day and seven days per week, so there is no time restriction limiting your learning. Besides, there is no space limit to inhibit you from studying; as a result, you can acquire the skills you need at your bed or bathroom. Also, assimilating the materials on YouTube is cost-effective, because most of the excellent education videos are entirely free. The most attractive feature of YouTube is that you can master the same topic from various instructors or YouTubers and all depend on your preferences. These reasons are why YouTube will replace traditional education methods within the next ten years (Tan, 2011).

No time and space limit for studying

If you just leave the office, do you want to spend 45 minutes or longer to take buses to go to classrooms at the opposite corner of downtown? The answer is “NO” for an exhausted worker and s/he just wants to go home to relax. Having the courses at a specific location discourages the students from performing their long-term study plan. Or, try to imagine that you have one-hour free time to gain the knowledge on a Wednesday afternoon, but there is no class from your academic institution at that time, meaning you will waste this hour to learn nothing. However, the YouTube platform is completely different from traditional educational methods. You can lie down on the sofa at your house and use your iPad to get the information about astronomy. Also, if you want to know how to do the gardening effectively “during midnight,” you can take out your cellphone and consult the YouTube videos (Rawal, 2018). These advantages of YouTube learnings give us more benefits during pandemic lock-down now. The students ranging from elementary school to the graduate school must stay home to avoid spreading the infections, so they can browse different videos on the platform to make up for not going to schools. They can sit at the desk to grasp the materials including science, literature and even calculus around the world. Moreover, the students can arrange the schedule in a day they desire to study from the channels, like 3 hours in the morning, 4 hours in the afternoon and 3 hours at night (Keskin, 2018). The brand-new learning approach is flexible for everyone and no restriction limits your study motivation, so the sky is limit.

A cost-effective educational application

A series of TOEFL or GRE courses at academic institutions will cost you about two thousand to three thousand Canadian dollars in Asia. This amount of investment is expensive for some students. Also, if you want to learn how to code using different programming languages or you want to pass the various exams held by CISCO, an industry leader, and get the certificate to find the jobs, you need to pay the cram school around 20 thousand for these courses at Japan (Human Academy Co., 2020). As a result, for some job seekers, they have to save enough money and think twice before they pay the tuition. Going to traditional institutions to study is a considerable expenditure for both students and adults. Nonetheless, you can search for the videos teaching you how to use the correct English grammar in ESL (English as a Second Language) courses on YouTube (Duffy, 2008). You do not need to pay a penny to acquire the knowledge of English writing on this platform. Furthermore, if you type “programming” in the search bar, you are going to have a million recordings about how to code with various languages and the instructors teach you the technologies step by step as in the campus (Siraj, 2013). They are all free also. For a beginner, you do not have to pay these YouTubers at all and you can develop the ability after studying videos. Not to mention the cost of commuting from the place you live to the building of the classes (Rawal, 2018). Consequently, why do not you use the YouTube channels to hone your skills with zero payment instead of enrolling at the traditional institutions with a massive cost?

Assorted and abundant choices

Last but not least, for a novice, you cannot distinguish who the best teacher is in this field and what kind of studying process you should take, so you choose one prestigious institution that may not be the best fit for us. It all depends on the luck and the learning path is not appropriate for the most beginners. Also, you all have the experience of having a famous instructor whose teaching style you hardly adapt to, but you have paid the tuition or registered for one term and you have no other choices to make us learn effectively as other peers. How should you do in this circumstance? You just stop learning most of the time (Lichter, 2012). Notwithstanding, you are not going to face these annoying difficulties when using YouTube. There are over 10 billion videos on the platform. You can access more than 10 thousand recordings for a single topic in a specific field. If you cannot understand what this YouTuber is talking or barely keep the pace with the teacher, you can change to another YOUTUBE IS A PROMISING INSTRUCTIONAL TOOL THAT WILL REPLACE TRADITIONAL EDUCATION METHODS WITHIN THE NEXT TEN YEARS channel to learn by one click. Besides, if the content of a series of videos is too hard or too easy for you, you could switch to different YouTubers and it is very easy (Rawal, 2018). Because there are a huge number of channels, you must get the videos to satisfy your own requirements on YouTube with ease. Everyone is different and everyone’s ability is not the same either, so gaining the knowledge you need on YouTube is the best choice for the people living in the advanced world (Siraj, 2013).

Conclusion

In conclusion, from the examples and evidence above, we know why YouTube is a promising instructional tool that will replace traditional education methods. Nowadays, people are much busier than before and everyone wants to accomplish the goals effectively and efficiently. The learning through YouTube removes all the time and space limits as traditional methods have. Besides, the cost-effectiveness is the first priority when the customer purchases the items; as a result, the free YouTube studying is the only choice they desire to have. Last, extremely numerous recordings on the platform can help each individual acquire the suitable resources and YouTube benefits all motivated students.
